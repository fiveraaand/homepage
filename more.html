Customer benefits

Convenience
The R5wallet app is data-free therefore the users won't have to worry about airtime and data bundles whenever they want to use the app, it also makes it easy to do transactions from the comfort of your home.
Ease of access
One of the primary benefits of Mobile Banking is that it has made it possible for you to stonewall the typical challenges of accessing your account once the bank's branch closes for the day. You do not have to wait for the bank to reopen the following day to transfer money to another account. You can easily access your account 24x7 and transfer funds whenever you need to.
More security
You can beef up the reliability of the financial transactions once you log into your Mobile Banking app by using a multi-level authentication system that requires the user to go through a pronged security set-up before managing the account.
Helps you track your finances
Managing your R5wallet account puts you in charge of your money and helps you gauge your financial standing.
