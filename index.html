About us

R5wallet is an all in one Cashless Mobile Payment Wallet application created to offer financial payment services in informal sectors. Let us help you reimagine the way you transact with the digital R5wallet to get ahead of the curve and solve your banking problems.


Founded in 2021, R5wallet specializes in digital-wallet banking in the financial sector. We work closely with all our clients to determine their individual banking needs.
